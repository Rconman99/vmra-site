## What to send

For each driver above, if you have any of these, send what you've got:

1. **One paddock photo** (car parked, hood up or down — daylight is best, side-3/4 angle ideal)
2. **One on-track photo** (race or hot-laps shot, even if from your phone)
3. **Sponsor list** — who's on the hood, doors, quarters
4. **Hometown** — city + state
5. **Years racing** — even just "since 2008" works
6. **One sentence** the driver wants on their card (their racing nickname, their motto, their dedication, whatever)

**Email everything to board@vmra.club** with the car number in the subject line
(e.g., "Car #30 Kyten Jones photos").

Don't worry about photo quality — phone shots are fine. We can crop, color-correct,
and resize. We'd rather have a phone snapshot of every car on the grid than wait
for a pro shoot that never happens.

---

## Image specs (for those who want them)

- **Minimum**: 1200 × 900 px (4:3 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPEG, HEIC, PNG — anything modern phones produce
- **No watermarks** unless you can send the original

We resize and convert to WebP/AVIF on our end for fast loading.
