# VMRA Weekly Race Update Template

**For the team:** Copy this template, fill in the bracketed sections after each race, and email it to **board@vmra.club** within 48 hours of the checkered flag. Ryan applies it to the website the same week. Don't worry about formatting — keep blanks blank if you don't have the info.

---

```
RACE: [date YYYY-MM-DD] · [track name] · Round [number]
For example: 2026-04-19 · Evergreen Speedway · Round 1

WINNER:
  #[car number] [Driver Name]
  Optional one-liner: [e.g., "took it on a broken upper control arm"]

FAST TIME:
  #[car] [Driver Name] · [time, e.g. 16.17]

PODIUM:
  1. #[car] [Driver Name]
  2. #[car] [Driver Name]
  3. #[car] [Driver Name]

POINTS UPDATE (top drivers after this race):
  #30 Kyten Jones    574  (was 539)
  #22 Steve Woods    559  (was 534)
  #23 Kahl Cheth     503  (was 488)
  #57 Shane Strimple [new] (was [old])
  ...etc — list as many as you have updated totals for

ATTENDANCE: [optional, e.g. "23 cars, ~400 in stands"]

CONDITIONS: [optional, e.g. "Wet qualifying, dried for feature, 58°F"]

NOTES: [optional — any storyline worth telling fans:
  who broke down, who came from the back, who passed who in turn 4,
  rookie debuts, milestones (100th start, etc.), incidents.
  Two or three sentences is plenty.]
```

## Quick rules

1. **One template per race.** Don't bundle two race weekends into one email.
2. **Use `#` before car numbers** (e.g. `#30`, not `30`). Helps the parser.
3. **Dates in YYYY-MM-DD** format (e.g. `2026-04-19`, not "April 19th").
4. **Bracketed prompts can be left out** — only fill in what you have.
5. **Even partial updates help.** Just the points + winner is enough; notes/attendance/conditions are bonus.
6. **Photos welcome separately.** Reply with photo attachments to the same thread; we'll get them on the racers page.

Questions? Email **board@vmra.club**.
