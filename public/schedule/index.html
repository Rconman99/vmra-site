§ 2026 · 40th Anniversary Tour

Apple Cup Done.
Ten More to Run.

Kahl Cheth #23 took the 57th running of the Apple Cup at Tri-City — main event winner by the full 25-point margin on a night the defending champ didn't unload. Up next: Grocery Outlet Night at Evergreen this Saturday, then Apple Blossom Rubber Down at Wenatchee the following weekend, the CARS Tour Mark Galloway Shootout in June, the Ron Rohde Memorial at Stateline (non-points) in July, the 40th Anniversary Bash at South Sound late July, and a four-race summer/fall sprint into the Fall Classic at Tri-City October 3-4. Eleven dates total. Nine for points. Two for the love of it.
