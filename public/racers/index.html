The Field.

These cars don't roll off a truck. They get built. Tube chassis. Fiberglass body. Small-block V8. Nothing on it that didn't get put there by the owner or someone who owed the owner a favor. This is what 40 years of vintage modified racing actually looks like — a car in a shop, a sponsor on the door, a number on the roof, a memory on the deck.
