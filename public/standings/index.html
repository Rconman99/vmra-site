The Book Kyten Defends.

Below is the 2025 final championship book — the title race the 40th Anniversary season picks up from. Five points separated Kyten #30 and Steve Woods #22 when the checkered dropped on Fall Classic night. Kahl Cheth #23x held third by 46. The 2026 season opened April 12 at Tri-City with Kyten taking the 57th Apple Cup; live 2026 YTD standings appear here after Round 02 at Evergreen (Apr 25). Points stay with the car, not the driver.
